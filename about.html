The Journey So Far

Recently, I discovered that I had an interest in all things tech and coding. Finding the confidence to challenge myself and step out of my comfort zone has been a welcome mindset change. Previously, I studied psychology and working a variety of odd jobs. These experiences have inherently shaped my life and have led me to where I am today. However, it really feels like I've turned a new page and started a new chapter.

History

I spent the last 6 years studying psychology, with a particular interest in industrial-organisational psychology. After spending a small amount of time in the industry, and becoming more acquainted with the pervasive nature of technology, I realigned my career aspirations. I've worked a variety of jobs - manufactured 3D figurines, served alcohol at ungodly hours and worked in an adult goods warehouse. I have a keen interest in understanding people, and I like to think the variety of work and study I've done has really shaped that. You can find my résumé by clicking the icon below.
